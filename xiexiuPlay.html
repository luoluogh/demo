【LESSON 9 - 10】 词：welcome, crowd, gather, hand, shout, refuse, laugh, jazz, musical, instrument, clavichord, recently, damage, key, string, shock, allow, touch

【LESSON 11 - 12】 词：turn, deserve, lawyer, bank, salary, immediately, luck, captain, sail, harbour, proud, important
